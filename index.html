SUSHANT SINGH RAJPUT

Tribute to SSR

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema.

Timeline of SSR's life:

  • 21 January 1986 SSR was born in Patna in the state of Bihar
  • 2008 SSR started his career after dropping out of his engineering course at the Delhi College of Engineering and entering the theatre industry in Mumbai.
  • 2009-2011 He moved on to feature in Hindi television serials,by the lead role in the soap opera Pavitra Rishta.
  • 2013 He made his first film debut with the film adaptation Kai Po Che! which became a commercial and critical success.
  • Apart from acting, he was the founder of a nonprofit organisation and has been credited as the co-founder of a technological startup.
  • He was deeply interested in astrophysics and won the National Olympiad in Physics.
  • 2020 His last film, Dil Bechara ,was released posthumously on the streaming platform Hotstar.
  • 14 June 2020 SSR, aged 34, was found dead, hanging from the ceiling fanin his home in Bandra, Mumbai.
